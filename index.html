About

I am an emerging museum professional with expertise in curatorial practice, collection management, and information systems. My work focuses on making cultural heritage accessible and engaging for diverse audiences through innovative programming and community engagement.

With experience spanning museums in both Canada and the UK, I bring a global perspective to cultural work while maintaining strong connections to local communities and their stories.
